## Glossary and Definitions

### Key Terms
- **Digital Twin**: A virtual representation of a physical system that mirrors the real-world system in real-time
- **Physics Simulation**: Computational modeling of physical laws to simulate realistic robot and environment behavior
- **Sensor Simulation**: Virtual representation of real sensors with realistic noise and characteristics
- **Real-time Synchronization**: Process of maintaining consistent state between simulation and visualization environments
- **Middleware**: Software layer that enables communication between different systems (e.g., ROS)
